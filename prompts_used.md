# AI Effectiveness Analysis

## What AI Did Well

ChatGPT was very effective at explaining CRM concepts, comparing products, and organizing information.

## What AI Struggled With

AI sometimes provided information that was too general and broad it didnt have specific details. 

## What Information Required Validation

CRM's popularity, any product pricing, and feature availability.

## What Surprised Me

I was surprised by the how many of CRM solutions are available and how many businesses rely on CRM software for sales, marketing, and customer support since this is the first time I'm hearing about it.

## What Would I Do Differently Next Time

Next time, I would start earlier in the week to spend more time comparing official CRM with AI responses to improve accuracy.

## Would I Trust AI For Software Research?

Yes, but only as a te,mplate. AI is nice and all for learning new topics quickly, but important information needs to always be verified using reliable sources.
